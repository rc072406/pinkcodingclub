Philosophy

Just be kind to others, even if they are completely different from you. Sometimes, spaces that try to increase inclusivity and diversity often times miss the mark because they put other groups down to lift themselves up. That's not what we're doing here. The girly girl and the tomboy (and guys!) can coexist in the tech world and work together to create amazing innovations! Twilight Sparkle in the Equestria Girls Cafeteria Song said it best: "I'm gonna be myself, no matter what I do. And if we're different, yeah, I want you to be true to you." So, you're a member of the Pink Coding Club as long as you can stay true to yourself AND be kind to others!

How to Join

No sign ups required! Just head on over to the curriculum to join the Pink Coding Club revolution! You can start learning by choosing whichever course you would like to start with and follow the instructions once you get there! After you finish the certification projects and required learning documentation, you will get a free certificate with the Pink Coding Club.
