01 // Why P.C.C was Made

Coding programs, even ones dedicated to girls only, have always appealed to the stereotypical tomboy. After hearing many anecdotes where girls have changed themselves to be less traditionally feminine to fit into tech spaces, the founder of P.C.C. said enough was enough and created a space where the girly girl is embraced and can be her full amazing and empowered self.
